Privacy Policy for Threadwise

Effective Date: May 25, 2026

Threadwise (“Threadwise,” “the app,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) is a clothing label and material analysis app designed to help users understand garment composition, care information, fabric behavior, and related clothing insights.

This Privacy Policy explains what information Threadwise collects, how that information is used, and what choices you have.

Summary: Threadwise is designed to be privacy-conscious. The app analyzes clothing label text and material information to provide garment insights. We do not sell your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

Threadwise may process the following types of information:

  • Clothing label text: Text captured through OCR scanning or manually entered by you, such as fiber composition, care instructions, country of origin, or related garment label details.
  • Garment details: Optional information you provide, such as garment type, brand, material composition, or notes about a clothing item.
  • Camera access: Threadwise may request access to your device camera so you can scan clothing labels. Camera access is used only to support label scanning.
  • Purchase status: If Threadwise offers paid features, one-time purchases, or subscriptions, the app may process purchase entitlement information through Google Play Billing to determine whether premium features should be unlocked.
  • Basic app diagnostics: Depending on the app version and services used, limited technical information may be processed to help maintain app performance, troubleshoot issues, or improve reliability.

2. Information We Do Not Intentionally Collect

Threadwise is not designed to collect:

  • Your name, unless you voluntarily provide it through a support request.
  • Your precise location.
  • Your contacts.
  • Your financial card details.
  • Sensitive personal information such as health, biometric, religious, political, or government identification data.

Payment details for purchases made through Google Play are handled by Google Play and are not directly collected or stored by Threadwise.

3. How We Use Information

Threadwise uses information to:

  • Analyze clothing label text and garment material composition.
  • Generate fabric, care, durability, pilling, and material insights.
  • Allow users to save or review garment scan results, if this feature is enabled.
  • Provide premium access when a valid purchase or subscription is detected.
  • Improve app functionality, reliability, and user experience.
  • Respond to support requests or user inquiries.

4. Local-First Design

Threadwise is intended to operate as a local-first app where practical. This means garment scans, material analysis, and saved clothing information may be stored on your device rather than on a Threadwise server.

If future versions add optional cloud sync, account features, analytics, or remote processing, this Privacy Policy will be updated to explain those changes.

5. Camera and OCR Processing

Threadwise uses camera access so you can scan clothing labels. Images captured for label scanning are used to extract text and help generate garment analysis.

Unless the app clearly tells you otherwise, label images and OCR results are processed for the purpose of providing the scan result and are not sold to third parties.

6. Saved Scans and Manual Entries

If Threadwise allows you to save scans or manually entered garments, that information may be stored locally on your device. You may be able to delete saved garment records from within the app.

Uninstalling the app may delete locally stored data, depending on your device settings and Android backup behavior.

7. Purchases and Subscriptions

Threadwise may offer paid access, including one-time purchases or subscriptions. Purchases made through the Android app are processed using Google Play Billing.

Threadwise may receive purchase entitlement information, such as whether a subscription or purchase is active, so that premium features can be unlocked. We do not receive your full payment card number from Google Play.

8. Third-Party Services

Threadwise may rely on third-party services that support app functionality, such as Google Play Billing, Android platform services, crash reporting, analytics, or OCR-related tools.

These services may process limited technical or usage information according to their own privacy policies. If additional third-party services are added in future versions, this Privacy Policy should be updated to identify them more specifically.

9. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share limited information only when necessary to:

  • Operate app functionality, such as purchase verification or platform services.
  • Comply with legal obligations.
  • Protect the app, users, or others from fraud, abuse, or security issues.
  • Respond to a user support request that you initiate.

10. Data Retention

Locally saved garment scans or manual entries may remain on your device until you delete them or uninstall the app.

Support emails or related correspondence may be retained for a reasonable period so we can respond to your request and maintain records of the issue.

11. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information processed by Threadwise. However, no app, device, or electronic storage method can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

12. Children’s Privacy

Threadwise is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

13. Your Choices

You may be able to:

  • Decline camera permission, although scanning features may not work without it.
  • Manually enter label information instead of using OCR scanning.
  • Delete saved garment scans or records from within the app, if the feature is available.
  • Manage or cancel subscriptions through your Google Play account.
  • Contact us with privacy questions or deletion requests.

14. International Users

Threadwise may be available to users in different countries. Privacy rights may vary depending on your location. If you contact us about a privacy request, we will make reasonable efforts to respond in accordance with applicable laws.

15. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will update the effective date above. Continued use of Threadwise after changes are posted means you accept the updated Privacy Policy.
